Animal in extinction

The sea turtle is a turtle that lives in the marine habitat, you can find them in a lot of sites but normally in Australia in tropical and subtropical climate waters. It is in danger of extinction, and if they extinct we can lose a lot of biodiversity because the turtle has very important jobs in the sea like helping maintain the health seagrass beds and coral reefs, which benefit commercially valuable species. There are 356 species of turtles and in Australia there are approximately 85.000 – 90.000 turtles, so they are of food depending on the species and stage of life, but in general their diet includes jellyfish, algae, crustaceans, molluscs, fish and other invertebrates.
